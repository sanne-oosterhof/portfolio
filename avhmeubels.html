AVH Meubels

  • Role: UX/UI design & design system
  • Duration: 3 months
  • Tools: Figma & Wordpress

Project

For AVH Meubels, a young company that creates custom furniture and interiors, I redesigned their outdated website into a fresh and modern look. The old orange and light blue color scheme was replaced with a bold combination of Makita blue, black and white, giving the brand a stronger and more professional identity. A new “Services” section was added to the homepage to clearly highlight what AVH Meubels offers, while existing sections were updated to match the new design. I also designed new category pages to present products in a clear and organized way, making it easier for customers to explore the full range. The result is a modern and user-friendly website that reflects the brand’s craftsmanship and passion for design.
