Root Agency

  • Role: UX/UI design, design system & style guide
  • Duration: 1 month
  • Tools: Figma

Project

For Root Agency, a young company focused on building sustainable Shopify webshops, I transformed a low-fidelity wireframe into a high-fidelity design that reflects both their values and visual identity. I set up a complete design system in Figma and created a compact style guide to maintain consistency and scalability. In addition to refining existing ideas, I designed new sections and interactions to enhance both usability and visual appeal. Special attention was given to elements like an interactive background and a playful Tetris concept, adding a dynamic and personal touch. With attention to usability and the agency’s sustainable identity, the final result is a clean, modern, and future-ready homepage design that brings their vision to life.
