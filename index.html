About me

Fresh out of university and full of energy, I am a digital and UX designer based in Rijsenhout. I graduated in June 2025 with a degree in Communication and Multimedia Design from the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. During my studies, I focused on UX and UI design, supported by minors in Visual Interface Design and Service Design. I enjoy designing digital experiences that are clear, user-friendly and visually refined. With a strong foundation in UX research, visual design and frontend development, I approach each project with structure and attention to detail. My goal is always to create intuitive and meaningful solutions that truly serve the user.
