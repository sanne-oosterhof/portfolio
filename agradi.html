Agradi

  • Role: UX/UI design & design system
  • Duration: 2 months
  • Tools: Figma & Wordpress

Project

During my internship at Surf&Turf Digital, I worked on redesigning Agradi, a large online equestrian and pet shop. The main challenge was their complex menu, which made it hard for users to find the right categories. After gathering inspiration and collaborating with the client, we developed a three-level expandable menu for desktop and mobile, improving navigation while keeping the existing brand style. I also redesigned key pages, including the homepage, collections, blog, cart, and FAQ, focusing on functionality, clarity, and consistency across the site. Throughout the project, I gained hands-on experience tackling a large-scale, multi-page redesign. The result is a refreshed, user-friendly webshop that feels modern, organized, and true to Agradi’s brand identity, making it easier for customers to explore and shop.
