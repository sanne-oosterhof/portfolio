Hittebingo

  • Role: UX/UI design, UX research, testing, ideation
  • Duration: 5 months
  • Tools: Figma & Adobe Illustrator

Project

For my graduation project, I designed Hittebingo, a digital interactive tool aimed at raising awareness among young people about the risks of extreme heat and motivating them to take practical precautions. Based on research and feedback, I created a concept that combines playful elements, personal stories, and a competitive twist to make learning about heat safety engaging and accessible. I independently handled the full process, including UX/UI design, user research, testing methods, and creative ideation. The result is an interactive and informative concept that encourages young people to understand and act on heat-related health risks in a fun and approachable way. I received a grade of 7.8 for my graduation project.
